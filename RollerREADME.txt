Made during version v.105

Made For World of Darkness 2e for exploding dice 10,9,8 and Rote.

Doesn't work with automatic updates
Roller is on client and web portal.

This is not an official ARES plugin, just a pet project.

Client commands:
'roll #' - roll # amount of dice, no explosions.
'roll/t #' - roll # amount of dice, explosions at 10
'roll/n #' - roll # amount of dice, explosions at 9 or 10
'roll/e #' - roll # amount of dice, explosions at 8, 9 or 10
'roll/s #' - roll # amount of dice, explosions at 7,8, 9 or 10
